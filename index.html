Featured Practice

AI Oversight & Administration

AI is arriving in government and in medicine whether organizations are ready or not. We help agencies, hospitals, and practices adopt it deliberately, with the policies, guardrails, and ongoing administration that public accountability and patient trust demand.

Govern

AI Governance & Policy

Acceptable use policies, risk frameworks, and review processes tailored to government and healthcare. Aligned with state and federal guidance and with HIPAA obligations, and written in plain language your workforce will actually follow.

Assess

AI Readiness & Use Case Assessment

A clear inventory of where AI genuinely helps your operation and where it does not. Prioritized by value, risk, and data readiness, not by vendor hype.

Evaluate

Vendor & Tool Evaluation

Independent assessment of AI products and of AI features being added to systems you already own: data handling, accuracy claims, procurement language, and exit strategy.

Administer

Ongoing Oversight & Administration

Standing administration of your AI program: usage monitoring, incident and exception review, staff guidance, and regular reporting to leadership and governing bodies.

Why it matters:

Public sector AI mistakes become public records. In healthcare, they become patient safety and privacy events. The organizations that benefit from AI will not be the ones that adopted it fastest. They will be the ones that administered it well.
